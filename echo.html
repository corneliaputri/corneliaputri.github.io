Meet Echo!

Dogs do speak, but only to those who know how to listen. -Orhan Pamuk

Hello everyone! My name is Echo. I started living with my owner when I was only 2 months old. I am in need of attention 24/7 and I love to play catch. I love being outdoor but I am still learning how to behave when I am outside of the house. I sleep a lot during the day but up most of the times at night guarding the house and making a bit of trouble inside the house. Regardless, my owner still loves me.
